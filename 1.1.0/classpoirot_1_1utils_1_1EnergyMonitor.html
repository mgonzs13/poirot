Detailed Description

Class for monitoring CPU energy consumption.

Provides methods for reading energy from RAPL, hwmon, or estimating from power measurements. Maintains state for tracking accumulated energy.

This method calculates the energy attributed to a specific thread based on its CPU time usage relative to the process and system.
